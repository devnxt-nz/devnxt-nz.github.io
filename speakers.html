Aimee ‘Max’ Whitcroft is founder and principal of data consultancy Data 4D, Vice-Chair of the Open Data Charter Advisory Board, and Senior Coordinator, Knowledge and Delivery at Open North.

She is an international advocate for what she calls #openX — open data, open government, civic tech, open access, open source and so on. She mixes these with her passion for privacy advocacy, data governance, ethics, futures thinking and strategy.

She's also seriously into plants and dogs :)
