This conference gives us all a rare opportunity to set aside the tools and technologies that can dominate our day-to-day work and concentrate on what inspires us look ahead to what’s next.

Come and listen to experienced, recognised speakers from around NZ and the world as they talk about making a meaningful impact with our fellow techies and the world at large

Take the opportunity to discuss with a broad range of people-minded people about what it means to be an whole-life IT person, and how we can encourage and support our communities to do the same

About the DevNxt conference

DevNxt is a full day of speakers and discussions around the concept of being your whole person in an IT profession.
