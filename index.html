A tech conference on the non-tech bits

Good, working software depends on people to nourish and sustain their teams and deliver great results. How do you create a team with the right blend of awareness, experience and capability?

Come along and hear from recognised industry professionals who will share their perspectives, observations and ideas, and meet other NZ industry professionals in a relaxed, open setting designed to help you connect, learn and level up your team and your career.

DevNxt Discussions

This year we're doing something a bit more...

Listening to the insights of fantastic speakers is a great way to spend a day, but this is a people-focused event. That means building relationships and spending time with each other.

At DexNxt Discussions spend two days in an exclusive smaller group with the speakers, building relationships and talking about whatever matters to you, at a mystery location in the Queenstown lakes area. This is run in an un-conference style to get the best out of the time.

About

DevNxt is a full day boutique conference in the beautiful Queenstown Lakes district, hearing from respected and recognised technical leaders on a range of topics that apply to all levels of your business technology capability. From senior developers, architects, team leaders and team members aspiring to move into a new role (or even those who are unsure if it’s the right thing for them), our speaker topics will provide a personal and insightful window into those next steps.

Conversations about developing stronger teams, cultures and looking ahead to what’s next
